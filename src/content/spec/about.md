# About Me

Hi, I’m Prajun. I’m a cybersecurity enthusiast with a curiosity for exploring systems, experimenting with tech, and learning how things really work.  

This site is a mix of my portfolio and my blog. Here, you’ll find:

- **Projects** – things I’ve built, tested, or hacked (ethically!)
- **Writeups** – notes, tutorials, and reflections from my experiments
- **Ideas & Thoughts** – random tech musings and observations

I believe in learning by doing and sharing knowledge along the way. Whether you’re here to check out my work, learn something new, or just explore, you’re welcome to stick around.  

Feel free to connect or reach out if something here sparks your interest!
